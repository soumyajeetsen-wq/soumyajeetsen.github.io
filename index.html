📊 NPS Deep Dive — Feb '26
  • STAR and LEADER are the healthiest seller segments — both at their best-ever NPS this month
  • Catalog trails all other segments by a wide margin — the gap is not closing meaningfully
  • TSCatalog is the only segment declining — the only one moving against the trend
  • Established sellers are significantly more satisfied than new sellers — the onboarding experience likely remains a pain point
  • New seller NPS jumped sharply in Feb — too early to confirm as a trend, needs March data

🔍 NPS Complaint Signals — Feb '26
  • Irrelevant Leads dominates seller complaints — it is the single biggest pain point in NPS feedback
  • Less BLs is rising fast — sellers are increasingly feeling undersupplied with enquiries
  • Low Conversion is a growing concern — sellers are getting leads but struggling to close them
  • Retail Leads and Wrong Category are the most cited reasons under Irrelevant Leads — specificity is improving
  • The sharp rise in total complaints in Feb reflects higher survey participation, not a sudden deterioration
